About This Project

This platform facilitates the systematic collection of data on direct democracy instruments, including citizen-initiated referendums and agenda initiatives at the national level.

Structured Questionnaires

Standardized forms ensure consistent data collection across countries and researchers.

Collaborative Platform

Multiple researchers can work simultaneously on assigned tasks with real-time updates.

Track Progress

Save drafts and track completion status for each assigned questionnaire.
